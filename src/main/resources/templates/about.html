About

The reason why it was decided to develop this type of project is because, now, people love comfort, many of us don’t want to left their comfort zones. We want to make our lives more comfortable, by developing this project, which will give us opportunity to buy simple grocery products without leaving home. Especially it will be actual in CIS countries. Our idea is to make a simple and well designed boxes, by which your product will be delivered. Purpose of this box to give you chance to not pay for delivery, you will pay only for first order. Also this boxes will decorate your home.
